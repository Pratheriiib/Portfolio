Trey's Web Maps

Here a few examples of the maps and webpages I have made throughout my time in the New Mapping Plus program at the University of Kentucky.

US Power Plants

This map shows all of the power plants in the United States that are generate power using Solar, Wind, or Hydro power.

Critical Habitat

This is a map that shows areas within the state of Kentucky that are listed as either Critical or Threated habitat by the US Fish & Wildlife Service.

The Daily Grind

This is a webmap that I made that demonstrates the route I take for my daily commute, as well as a few places I often stop at along the way.

Bars and Restaurants Near Lexington Parks

This web page features a map made in QGIS using Open Street Maps Data. This map shows Streets, Buildins, and Parks in Lexington. In addition, It highlights any bars or restaurnats within one mile (walking distance) of those parks.

Hawaiian Craters

This is a web page I made featuring a Mapbox map fixed on a Hawaiian crater called Ka'au. If you zoom out, it shows the rest of the known craters in Hawaii

Classic Raisz Map Overlaid with Appalachian Trail

This project is a web page displaying a classic Erwin Raisz landforms map that has been georeferenced in QGIS and then overlaid with a shapefile of the Appalachian Trail.

About me

Hi! I'm Trey Prather and I am a forester with a diverse mapping skill set. I am comfotable with the ESRI platform of software as well as QGIS. In addition to traditional geospatial analysis, my time in the New Mapping Plus program at UK has taught me everything from database management using PostGIS and Spatialite, to web mapping and site design using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I also have learned to use such web mapping databases such as Carto, Leaflet, and MapBox!

When I'm not in the woods or behind a computer screen you can catch me hanging out with my wife Kennedy and my daughter Juniper. I also enjoy playing disc golf, fantasy football, and softball with my friends. My other hobbies include enjoying the outdoors through hiking or fishing, and playing guitar!
